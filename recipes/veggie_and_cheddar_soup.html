Homemade Veggie and Cheddar Soup from Scratch

Description

This is a quick and easy recipe that tastes absolutely amazing!

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of thick cut bacon

  • 5 cups chicken bone broth

  • 5 golden potatoes cut in 1/4ths

  • 2 cups frozen cauliflower

  • 1 small yellow onion, diced

  • 5 large garlic cloves minced

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour

  • 1 cup half and half

  • 1 bay leaf

  • 4 cups frozen broccoli

  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

  • Green onion, chopped

  • Ground black pepper

  • Salt

Preparation Instructions

  1. Heat a Dutch oven to medium heat

  2. Microwave half and half for 45 seconds to help keep it from curdling

  3. Cut strips of bacon into 1-inch pieces

  4. Place pieces of bacon into pot and stir occasionally until fully cooked

  5. Once fully cooked, remove from pot and place onto napkins on plate

  6. Put in potatoes, onion, and garlic into left over bacon grease in the Dutch oven

  7. Saut√©e until potatoes are mostly cooked through

  8. Stir in flour and mix together well

  9. Add remaining ingredients except cheese, bacon, and green onions

  10. Bring to boil, then lower temperature to low and simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally

  11. Serve immediately and top with cheddar cheese, bacon, and green onions
