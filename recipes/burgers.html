Dan's Famous Burgers

Description

This is Dan's Famous Burger! Below you will find the ingredients needed. I prefer to make it a double because I am a huge fan of beef but this can easily be made a single as well. I hope you enjoy your finished product, you can't really go wrong with a burger, just make sure you don't burn it!

Ingredients

  • Burger Buns '12ct.'

  • Ground chuck '2 lbs'

  • Bacon

  • Cheese

  • Lettuce

  • Tomato

  • Onions

  • Mayonaise

  • Mustard

  • 'Ketchup optional'

  • Butter

  • Salt and Pepper

Preparation Instructions

  1. Cook bacon in the oven or over the stove

  2. Start the fire on your grill

  3. Form the meat into a patty shape. I prefer doing 6 oz patties, but if you want something a little smaller do 4 oz patties

  4. Put the patties on a baking sheet and sprinkle salt and pepper on them

  5. Cut up the lettuce, tomatos, and onions to your liking

  6. Start the broiler on your oven

  7. Butter the buns and put them in the oven until buns are golden

  8. Take the buns out and set to the side

  9. Cook the patties

  10. Add condiments to the buns

  11. Add cheese to patties then take off the grill once cooked and cheese is slightly melted

  12. Add the patty to the bun, then add lettuce, tomato, onion, and bacon to the burger

  13. Serve!
